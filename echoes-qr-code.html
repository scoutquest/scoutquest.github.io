A Quest to Restore the Timeline

Key moments in Tennessee’s history are disappearing from our collective memory— and Bicentennial Park holds the clues to restore them. Search the grounds, solve the puzzles, and save Tennessee’s story before it slips away completely.

Get Started

Download the Scout Quest Companion App to begin your journey.

Already have the app installed?

  1. Open the Scout Quest Companion App on your device.
  2. Tap the "Add Quest" button on the main screen.
  3. Scan the QR code on your welcome card.

Having trouble scanning the QR Code?

Click "Enter Code Manually" and type in the quest code quest-echoes-984732
