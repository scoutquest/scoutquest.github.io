How It Works

The Quest Box One physical box with everything your group needs to complete 8 puzzles on location—built for collaboration, observation, and satisfying “aha” moments.
  • Designed for one group to share
  • Puzzle components are mostly physical (not screen-based)
The Place Designed for a specific Nashville park. Signs, monuments, and details become part of the puzzle—your surroundings provide the clues.
  • Explore with fresh eyes (the “answer” is often in plain sight)
  • Observation matters as much as logic
The Companion App (free) The app keeps your quest moving—confirm answers, track progress, and use hints if you need them. The solving happens in the real world.
  • One phone per group is enough
  • Hints are available (and affect your final time)

Launch Day

Echoes of Tennessee launches March 14, 2026. If you choose Launch-Day Pickup, you’ll pick up your Quest Box at the park and set out to solve the puzzles alongside other groups doing the same.

  • Play alongside other teams exploring, searching, and piecing things together
  • Be among the first to complete Echoes of Tennessee
  • Exclusive launch-day swag only available to launch participants

Can’t make it? No problem. Choose shipping and play later. Your group’s run is still timed in the app, and the Quest Box is exactly the same.
