About the Scout Quest Companion App

Discover how the Scout Quest Companion App enhances your adventure experience and organizes your digital clues.

How It Works:

Register for an Adventure

Secure your spot in a quest by signing up at scoutquest.co.

Scan Your Quest QR Code

On the adventure day, you'll receive a QR Code. Use the 'Add Quest' button in the app to scan and add your quest.

Follow the Clues

The app will keep track of your digital clues and progress as you uncover the mysteries of your quest.

Need Assistance?

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please don't hesitate to contact us at help@scoutquest.co.
