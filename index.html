FAQ

Scout Quest creates real-world puzzle adventures that blend escape-room style challenges with outdoor exploration. Each quest leads you through a location using clues you can discover on-site and puzzles you solve as a team.

Join our email list to get launch details, quest box updates, and new live event announcements. When reservations or tickets open, subscribers get notified first.

Currently, all Scout Quest adventures take place in Nashville, and each quest has its own location. Location details for new quests are shared with subscribers and quest box reservations first.

Scout Quest is designed for teams of friends, families, visitors, and groups looking for something different to do in Nashville. If you enjoy puzzles and exploring together, you’ll fit right in.

Not at all. You’ll be walking, observing, reading signs or markers, and solving puzzles as a team—no prior puzzle experience required.

Most quests take about 2–3 hours, depending on your pace and how deep you go on each puzzle.

We recommend teams of 3–6 for the best experience, though smaller groups or individuals can still play.

Each team plays independently. For live events, multiple teams may be out at the same time, but you won’t be solving puzzles collaboratively with other groups.

Bring water, wear comfortable shoes, and make sure your group has at least one phone for any digital check-ins or hints. Everything else you need is provided as part of the quest materials.
